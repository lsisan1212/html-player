💬 Subtitles

Click 💬 to load .srt or .vtt files. MKV embedded subtitles are auto-detected.

Use the CC dropdown to switch between subtitle tracks. Press V to cycle 10 subtitle styles.

🤖 AI Commentator

Click 🤖 to open AI settings. Select a language, paste your OpenRouter API key, and the AI will react to movie dialogue in real-time.

Default model: Llama 3.2 3B (fast & cheap). You can also choose Qwen 2.5, GPT-4o Mini, Claude, or any custom model.

⚠️ Must run via http://localhost — not file://. Use python3 -m http.server 8000 to start a local server.

🎨 10 Player Themes

Click 🎨 to switch between: Default, Dark, Light, Ocean, Forest, Sunset, Purple, Midnight, Neon, Minimal.

🔊 Volume

Adjust 0–100% with the slider or mouse wheel. Press M to mute/unmute.

📸 Screenshot

Click 📸 or press S to capture the current frame as a PNG download.
